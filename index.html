About Us

  • Mission
  • Vision
  • Value

Our mission is to empower individuals and businesses alike by providing accessible, reliable, and tailored legal solutions. We are committed to being a trusted partner on our clients' legal journeys, offering guidance, support, and expertise every step of the way. We believe that everyone deserves access to high-quality legal services, regardless of their background or circumstances. Our mission is to break down barriers and make the legal process understandable, transparent, and efficient. By doing so, we aim to alleviate the stress and uncertainty that often accompanies legal matters.

Our vision is to revolutionize the legal industry by redefining the way legal services are delivered. We aspire to be the leading provider of innovative, client-centric legal solutions that are accessible, efficient, and empowering. We envision a future where individuals and businesses can easily access the legal support they need, without the traditional barriers of complexity, high costs, and limited transparency. We strive to create an environment where our clients feel empowered, informed, and confident in navigating the legal landscape.

Exchange Legal Services started its operation in late 2022. The firm was founded by Mr. Ben Veerapen, a qualified Solicitor of the Supreme Court of England and Wales, and co-partner Hrushi Punjani. Although a relatively new firm, Ben Veerapen has been in legal practice since December 2002. Since its inception, Exchange Legal Services has hit the floor running with a following and already boasts a large database of clients ranging from the banking industry, overseas enterprises, and small to medium property developers..
